Our Sundance also welcomes support in the form of money donations. Each year, we rely on the generosity and accountability of our sundancers, helpers, community members, and our extended families to provide enough support to keep the Sundance going. We can donate to the Eagle Sundance fund securely through PayPal via the "Donate" button below. You can donate any amount any time in a single transaction ~or~ you're given the option to setup your donations on a monthly basis - that way you're able to donate a little each month over the course of our Sundance year. 100% of these collected donations will be used for Sundance related costs and supplies.

This PayPal donation button is intended solely for use by our Eagle Sundance family and its supporters as a way to simplify the process of donating to our Sundance. Miigwech (Thank you) for your support!
